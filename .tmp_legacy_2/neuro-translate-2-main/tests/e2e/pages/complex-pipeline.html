Chapter Nine: The Ledger Room

A rainstorm shook the windows. The clerk opened the ledger and read each entry twice, first for accuracy, then for fear. Every line was dated, signed, and impossible to explain.

“Do not translate the map labels literally,” said Vera. “They are idioms from the old river guild, and literal wording will make the route look absurd.”
